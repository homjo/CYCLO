Problems

  • Hard to find like-minded cyclists

  • No clear goal or sense of achievement

  • Track progress and share achievements

About Us

Our mission is to make cycling more fun and social

We are a group of developers who love cycling and have found that many cycling enthusiasts have given up this beneficial sport for both the body and mind due to a lack of motivation and social elements.

So, we created a cycling adventure park, introducing gamification elements into the cycling experience and turning every ride into an interesting adventure.

Our platform not only helps you maintain your exercise routine, but also enables you to make like-minded friends, explore new routes together and challenge yourself.
